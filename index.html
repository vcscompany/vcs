About Us


Founded on March 21, 2026, we are a nimble team of three dedicated founders—John Paul Clarito, John Francis Samillano, and James Angelo Viesca—based in San Jose, Antique, Philippines, passionate about powering the builders of tomorrow. At our core, we're more than a construction supply company; we’re partners in building the future, one project at a time. Sourcing premium materials, tools, and innovative solutions for contractors, developers, and visionaries, we deliver reliability, quality, and competitive value to fuel your next big build. From foundational steel and sustainable lumber to cutting‑edge equipment, our curated supply chain supports residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects with speed and precision. With a startup spirit and industry expertise, we're eager to collaborate, scale with you, and construct lasting success. Let's partner up and build the future together from San Jose, Antique.


Mission


To deliver top-quality construction supplies with unmatched speed and unwavering reliability, empowering builders, contractors, and projects to succeed on time and exceed expectations every single time.


Vision


To be the most trusted leader in the construction supply industry, where quality materials arrive fast and reliably, revolutionizing how projects are built efficiently and durably across every community we serve.


Company Goals


• Operational Excellence: To establish the fastest and most reliable supply chain network in the local construction industry.
• Market Leadership: To become the primary “top-of-mind” supplier for contractors and builders across all served communities.
• Customer Success: To empower clients by ensuring zero project delays caused by material shortages or delivery issues.


Objectives


• Speed & Efficiency: Achieve a 98% on-time delivery rate for all construction supply orders within the next 12 months.
• Quality Assurance: Ensure that 100% of products sourced and delivered meet or exceed national industry safety and durability standards.
• Community Expansion: Expand supply operations to three new communities or regions within the next two fiscal years to increase accessibility for local builders.
• Customer Trust: Maintain a customer satisfaction rating of 4.5/5 or higher, specifically focusing on reliability and communication during the fulfillment process.
• Partnership Growth: Secure formal supply partnerships with at least 10 major local contracting firms by the end of the year to solidify market presence.


Core Values


• Safety: The highest priority, ensuring a safe environment for employees, clients, and partners.
• Integrity & Honesty: Operating with transparency, maintaining ethical standards, and honoring commitments.
• Quality & Excellence: Providing superior products and high-quality service, ensuring excellence in all tasks.
• Customer Service & Satisfaction: Dedicated to fulfilling customer needs and building lasting relationships.
• Accountability & Responsibility: Holding oneself accountable for results, safety, and project deadlines.
• Teamwork & Collaboration: Fostering a respectful, united, and collaborative workplace.
• Reliability: Demonstrating consistency and dependability in service and product delivery.
